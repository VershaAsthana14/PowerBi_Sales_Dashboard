This is my Sales Performance Dashboard created using Microsoft Power BI, designed to provide a clear and interactive view of business performance.

At the top, we have key KPI cards showing Total Sales, Total Profit, Total Quantity, and Profit Margin. These KPIs give a quick snapshot of overall performance and help in understanding how the business is performing at a glance.

Moving to the trend analysis, the line chart displays sales over time, allowing us to identify patterns, seasonality, and growth trends. This helps in tracking whether sales are increasing or declining month over month.

On the right side, we have a Top Products bar chart, which highlights the top 5 products based on sales. The use of color gradients helps quickly identify high-performing products compared to others.

Below that, we have a regional or customer segment analysis, which shows how sales are distributed across different regions or customer categories. This helps in identifying key markets and potential areas for growth.

We also have interactive slicers for Date, Region, and Product Category. These allow users to filter the dashboard dynamically and drill down into specific insights.

Additionally, the dashboard includes growth metrics such as Sales Growth and Profit Growth percentage, which indicate business performance compared to previous periods.

Overall, this dashboard helps stakeholders make data-driven decisions by providing insights into sales trends, product performance, and regional contribution in a simple and visually effective way.
